"Why I want to become a developer?"

Passion for Technology: Many people are naturally drawn to technology and enjoy working with computers, software, and programming languages. Becoming a developer allows them to pursue their passion and work in a field that aligns with their interests. Problem Solving: Developers often face complex challenges and problem-solving is a fundamental aspect of their job. If you enjoy finding creative solutions to problems and puzzles, a career in development can be very rewarding. Creativity: Developing software and applications allows for creativity in design and functionality. Developers have the opportunity to create new and innovative solutions that can have a significant impact on people's lives.
